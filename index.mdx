TM:PE (Traffic Manager: President Edition) is an open-source mod for Cities: Skylines that replaces the game's simplistic traffic rules with a comprehensive set of tools. From setting custom speed limits on individual lanes to programming timed traffic lights and controlling which vehicle types can use each road, TM:PE puts you firmly in charge of your city's traffic network.

## Getting started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Install TM:PE">
    Subscribe to [TM:PE v11 STABLE](https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=1637663252) on Steam Workshop, or download binaries for non-Steam installs. See the [Installation guide](/installation) for full instructions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enable the mod">
    In Cities: Skylines, open **Content Manager → Mods** and enable **TM:PE**. The mod activates automatically when you load a city.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the toolbar">
    While in-game, click the TM:PE button in the road toolbar (or press the configured hotkey) to open the traffic management panel.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start optimizing">
    Select any traffic tool from the panel and click on roads or intersections in your city. Changes take effect immediately and are saved with your city.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  TM:PE is only supported on the latest version of Cities: Skylines. Always keep both the game and the mod updated for the best experience.
</Note>
