---
title: "What is TM:PE? Traffic management for Cities: Skylines"
description: "TM:PE is an open-source Cities: Skylines mod that replaces basic traffic rules with precise per-lane controls for speed limits, traffic lights, and routing."
---

TM:PE (Traffic Manager: President Edition) is a free, open-source mod for Cities: Skylines that gives you granular control over road and rail traffic in your city. Instead of relying on the game's built-in, hands-off traffic simulation, you can set speed limits on individual lanes, program timed traffic lights, define which vehicles may use each road, control turning movements at every junction, and much more. TM:PE has been actively developed since March 2015 — just four days after Cities: Skylines launched — and is one of the most widely used mods in the Cities: Skylines community.

## Key features

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Speed limits" icon="gauge-high">
    Override the default speed limit on any lane or road segment, in km/h or mph. Changes apply instantly and persist with your city save.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Lane arrows" icon="arrows-left-right">
    Control which directions vehicles may turn from each lane at intersections, eliminating unwanted lane changes and wrong-way detours.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Lane connectors" icon="code-fork">
    Manually connect source lanes to specific destination lanes for precise routing at complex junctions and highway on-ramps.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Timed traffic lights" icon="traffic-light">
    Place, remove, or program timed traffic lights with custom step sequences, durations, and vehicle-count–sensitive adaptive timing.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Priority signs" icon="triangle-exclamation">
    Assign yield, stop, or main-road priority at any junction so that vehicles follow the right-of-way rules you define.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Junction restrictions" icon="ban">
    Toggle u-turns, pedestrian crossings, mid-block lane changes, and blocked-junction rules on a per-intersection basis.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Vehicle restrictions" icon="truck">
    Limit road or lane access to specific vehicle types — for example, bus-only lanes or roads that ban heavy cargo trucks.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Parking restrictions" icon="square-parking">
    Disable roadside parking on specific segments to keep traffic moving and prevent gridlock near busy areas.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Community and resources

TM:PE is developed by volunteers and maintained in the open. All the links you need are below:

- **Steam Workshop** — [TM:PE v11 STABLE](https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=1637663252) and [TM:PE v11 TEST](https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=2489276785)
- **Discord** — Real-time chat, bug help, and feature discussion at [discord.gg/faKUnST](https://discord.gg/faKUnST)
- **GitHub** — Source code, issue tracker, and contributing guide at [CitiesSkylinesMods/TMPE](https://github.com/CitiesSkylinesMods/TMPE)
- **Bug reports** — Follow the [Report a Bug](https://github.com/CitiesSkylinesMods/TMPE/wiki/Report-a-Bug) guide so the team can help you efficiently

<Tip>
  If you experience vehicles mysteriously disappearing from your road network, use the community-recommended [Broken Node Detector](https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=1777173984) mod to find and fix the underlying game-engine bugs that cause traffic despawning.
</Tip>
